Working at the border of Printmaking and Fiber Art in Seoul, Korea.

Explore the relationship between texture and narrative, weaving stories through fibers such as wood and paper.
Inspired by nature, Works reflect the tension between stability and fragility, eventually expanding to life and death.
It captures the essence of nature in darkness and finds the quiet serenity within. Each piece reflects the beauty and struggle that exist only in that moment, questioning the essence of our lives. The process challenges the boundaries, expanding the dialogue between  images.
